Questions & Answers

When is the RSVP deadline?

Please RSVP by 30th August, so we can have an accurate headcount.

Are kids welcome?

Yes, kids are very welcome. During the ceremony the church has a family room where you can take the kids and still be able to participate in the ceremony.

Where should I park?

There is plenty of free parking at the rear of the church. Just drive down the left hand side of the church to the back.

The Olive Kitchen & Bar also has plenty of parking.

What should I wear?

There's no specific dress code—we'll leave it up to you to wear whatever makes you feel comfortable.

How long is the drive from the church to the reception?

The drive is about 30 minutes.

When can we take photos with the bride & groom?

There will be plenty of opportunity to take photos with the bride and groom at the reception.

How long is the Nuptial Mass?

The Nuptial Mass is expected to last for 1 hour.
